## Are apps built with Nxenv GDPR compliant?

**They can be! You can build GDPR compliant apps with Nxenv if you follow the requirements.**

While Nxenv ensures the proper handling and processing of developer data and end-user data, we cannot guarantee that the developers who build apps with Nxenv follow similar data privacy practices themselves.
